WARZONE WORLD SERIES 2021

An awesome tournament where we declare the best warzone squad and have fun while doing it. We have squads with people from different countries around the world. It is taking place in November, in Japan

2021.11.15(MON) ~ 19(FRI)

@ Activision, Sweden

Main Program

  • Schedule

    Listen to the communication involving the activities and the rules and regulations for the matches. We shall also talk about the order of the tasks

  • Exhibition

    Take a look at some of the greatest activision has to offer as well as history of activision. View our gallery of older games as well as past achievements

  • Social

    Move around and find new members for your squad and long term gaming partners. You may even get contracts with awesome gaminng crews such as optic gaming

  • Tournament

    The matches begin. The different teams shall be in their gaming stations and begin the battle for the best.

  • Winners

    We shall be declaring the winner and handing in prices for best teams. Other prizes will be given as well such as player with most K/D awards, most kills award and so on
